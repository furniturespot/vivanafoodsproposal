Executive Summary & Objectives

This proposal is designed for Vivana Foods Pvt Ltd to acquire high-quality international B2B customers and establish a strong global brand presence.

SPARK GEN MEDIA will act as a long-term digital growth partner by building a full-funnel digital ecosystem focused on trust, authority, and scalable demand generation.

B2B food brands face long sales cycles, strict compliance expectations, and high trust requirements. Our objective is to position Vivana Foods Pvt Ltd as a credible global supplier while driving consistent, high-intent B2B leads across multiple international markets.

Website Development Proposal

The Vivana Foods website will be the central hub for all B2B acquisition, built on the principle of high conversion, trust, and global SEO readiness.

  • High-Conversion Design: Focused on global buyers with clear CTAs, lead capture systems, and a clean, professional user experience.
